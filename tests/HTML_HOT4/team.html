Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs co-founded Apple in 1976, transforming the technology landscape with his innovative vision. Known for his passion for design and relentless pursuit of excellence, Jobs introduced revolutionary products like the iPhone, iPad, and Mac, shaping the future of technology. His creativity and commitment to creating elegant, user-friendly devices continue to inspire Apple's mission today.

Tim cook

Tim Cook, Apple CEO since 2011, has guided the company to new heights, expanding its product and service offerings while championing sustainability, privacy, and inclusivity. His leadership continues to build on Apple's legacy of innovation and excellence.
