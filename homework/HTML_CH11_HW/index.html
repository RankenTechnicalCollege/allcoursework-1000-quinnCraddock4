Winston

Winston is a brilliant scientist and a genetically engineered gorilla from the Horizon Lunar Colony. Known for his intelligence and strength, Winston uses his powerful jump pack and Tesla Cannon to fight for a better world.

Tracer

Tracer is a time-jumping adventurer and former Overwatch agent. With her ability to blink through space and rewind time, Tracer is a highly agile fighter who outmaneuvers her enemies at lightning speed.

D.Va

D.Va, a former professional gamer, pilots a state-of-the-art mech to protect her homeland. Armed with twin Fusion Cannons and defense matrix technology, she excels at both offense and defense in combat.
